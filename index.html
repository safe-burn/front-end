SAFETY FIRST


Autonomous yield and liquidity generation protocol. Simply hold SAFEBURN in your wallet and you will get more. On each transaction the protocol automatically distributes rewards to holders as well as auto-locks liquidity.

About

$SAFEBURN is programmed to reward holders while increasing in both liquidity and value.


It does this by applying a

4% tax on transactions
2% goes to holders (instantly and gaslessly)
2% is auto-locked to liquidity (forever)


Forever Deflationary supply/burn

We burned 50% of the total supply after launch and sent it to a black hole address; as this address also participates in the protocol, it accumulates more tokens, thereby effectively removing them from circulation. There is no limit to the burn, the black hole will keep growing, increasing the scarcity of SAFEBURN.


Auto-locking liquidity

2% of each transaction is locked to liquidity forever. This contributes to less volatility and a continuously increasing price floor.

100% safe

LP tokens were burned thereby locking initial liquidity away forever. Fair distribution without whales. Active and engaged team, ready to answer any questions.
